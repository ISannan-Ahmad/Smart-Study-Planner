About StudyPlanner

StudyPlanner is an intelligent application designed to help students organize their study sessions more effectively. Our mission is to reduce study stress and improve learning outcomes through smart planning and analytics.

How It Works

  1. Add your subjects and break them down into topics
  2. Log your study sessions to track time spent
  3. Receive AI-powered recommendations on what to study next
  4. View analytics to understand your study patterns
